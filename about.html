About me

I am a game and XR developer skilled in creating immersive AR and VR experiences. Using Unity as my primary engine, I’ve built AR applications leveraging ARCore, ARKit, Vuforia, and AR Foundation, as well as VR applications with OpenXR and the Meta Quest SDK. My background in game programming underpins these projects, enabling me to craft interactive simulations and visualization tools for sectors like medical training and real estate. My portfolio includes VR simulations for surgical training and virtual property tours, demonstrating a strong grasp of Unity and Unreal Engine.


I focus on practical problem-solving and continuous learning, and I’m open to opportunities in both game development studios and enterprise XR. I prioritize clear communication of my technical strengths and project experiences, following advice to tell a concise professional story.
